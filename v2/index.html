Closing Techniques (8)
▼
1. TENTATIVE APPOINTMENT — most common win
"I can put you on our calendar tentatively — no payment today, no commitment. You think it over, talk to your spouse, do your research. If anything changes, just give us a call 24 hours before and we'll remove you from the schedule. Does that sound fair?"
Best for: "need to think about it" / spouse objection. Lowest commitment threshold.
2. MONTH-TO-MONTH COUNTER — removes all commitment
"I can offer month-to-month — $49 install instead of $99, and you can cancel anytime. No commitment at all. That way you're testing it for literally the lowest possible cost. If you love it — and most people do — you just keep it going."
Best for: contract-averse leads. Removes all commitment barriers.
3. COMPLIMENTARY SITE SURVEY — for equipment doubters
"Let me schedule a free site survey. Our technician will come out, test the signal strength at your location, show you exactly where the small receiver would go, and answer any questions. No charge, no commitment. If you like what you see, we can install same-day. How does that sound?"
Best for: equipment concerns, spouse-dependent, technical buyers. Gets a tech on-site.
4. TRIAL CLOSE
"Based on what you've told me, the [plan] would be perfect for your household. You get unlimited data, no contracts, price locked, and local support. Plus the free TV service and 30-day money-back guarantee. Sound like what you're looking for?"
Best for: leads who have expressed interest. Use after Value Match. Listen for verbal cues.
5. FIRST MONTH FREE
"Your first month of service is completely free. So all you'd need to take care of is the one-time installation fee on the day of the appointment. That way you can test everything out — streaming, gaming, working from home — and see the difference for yourself before you're paying a dime for monthly service."
Best for: price-conscious leads. Creates a "nothing to lose" frame.
6. 30-DAY GUARANTEE
"You have 30 days to test everything out. Run both side by side and YOU decide which one you want to keep. If we don't blow them away, we come get the equipment and refund every penny. You literally can't lose."
Best for: hesitant leads, risk-averse buyers. Reframes install as TEST not SWITCH.
7. DISCOUNT CLOSE
"Let me check with our billing team... I can get you a $10/month discount if we lock this in today. That brings your monthly down even further. And remember, the price is locked — it never goes up."
Best for: leads sold on service but stalling on price. Use as LAST resort — don't lead with it.
8. SAVINGS MATH CLOSE
"You're paying $[current] now. Our plan is $[plan]. That's $__ savings per month, $__ per year. And the price never goes up. The math just works."
"Here's how I'd look at it — you're getting unlimited data, free TV service, and a price that's locked forever. [Provider] WILL raise your rate — they always do. When that bill goes up in 6 or 12 months, you'll be glad you made the switch now."
Best for: price shoppers, analytical buyers. When plan costs more than current, pivot to value + rate-hike framing.

Technology Explainer

30-Second Script: "What is UnFiber?"
"Great question! So we use something called UnFiber — it's our fixed wireless technology. Basically, we put a small receiver on the outside of your house — it's about the size of a laptop — and it connects to one of our local towers nearby. We own our towers — we're not piggybacking off other providers. Think of it like getting cable internet, but instead of digging up your yard, we beam the signal directly to your house. It's a dedicated connection just for you. Professional install, takes about an hour, and you're up and running. Plus you get free TV service and a router included."
What We Are NOT
NOT Satellite — No dish, no 600ms latency, not affected by rain/clouds. Our towers are local, not in space.
NOT Cellular/Hotspot — This is a dedicated connection, not your phone's data. You're not competing with mobile users for bandwidth.
NOT a Dish — The receiver is flat, about the size of a laptop. Mounts on the wall, eave, or small pole. No big ugly dish.
